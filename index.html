Welcome to QUEEN'S YUMMY

Hello! I'm QUEEN CYUBAHIRO, the founder of QUEEN'S YUMMY. With over 5 years of culinary experience across Europe and Asia, I've created a dining experience that combines traditional recipes with modern twists.
My passion for cooking started in my grandmother's kitchen, and now I'm excited to share these flavors with you.

My interest in web development grew from wanting to share our restaurant's story online. I believe that just like a perfectly plated dish, a website needs the right ingredients - HTML for structure, CSS for presentation, and JavaScript for interactivity.
This website represents my journey into the digital world while staying true to my culinary roots.
